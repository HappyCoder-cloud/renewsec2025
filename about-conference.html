    • About Conference

    RENEWSEC 2025 conference will bring together Top Industry Experts, Policymakers, CISOs, OT Engineers, Power Plant Operators, Cybersecurity Solution Providers & Thought Leaders to discuss the current challenges, best practices, and innovative solutions to strengthen the OT CyberSecurity of Renewable Energy Sector.

    Why Attend?

    Connect with global leaders in OT cybersecurity and Renewable Energy Sector

    Gain insights on the latest threat intelligence and incident response strategies.

    Explore real-world case studies and best practices from leading Renewable Energy Companies.

    Engage with key Policymakers, Regulators, and Technical Experts.

    Experience the latest cybersecurity solutions in the exhibition zone.

    Who Should Attend?

    • Top Management of RE & Clean Energy Companies
    • Decision Makers from Regulators, Policy Makers & Industry Associations
    • OT/ICS Cybersecurity Heads
    • NLDC, SLDC & RLDC Officials
    • Plant & Utility Operators
    • Risk & Compliance Managers
    • Government & Regulatory Agencies
    • Renewable Energy Developers
    • AI & Cybersecurity Solution Providers
    • Head of Renewable Projects / Assets
    • Academics and R&D experts in ICS Security & AI

    Key Topics to be Covered

    Regulatory and Policy Frameworks
    AI-Powered Cybersecurity: Advanced Threat Detection and Response
    Future Roadmap: OT Secured Renewable Energy Grid of 2030
    OT Cybersecurity Risk Management for Solar, Wind & Hydro Assets
    AI in OT Cybersecurity: Opportunities & Threats
    Securing the Convergence of IT & OT in RE Infrastructure
    Cybersecurity Challenges in Integration with Grid
    Building Cyber Resilience in DISCOMs & SLDCs
    Third-Party Risk Management in RE Supply Chains
    Capacity Building and Workforce Readiness
    Lessons from Cyber Incidents in Energy Sector: Global & Indian Case Studies
    Cyber Insurance for OT Assets in RE Sector
    OEMs & Utilities Dialogue: Building a Security Assurance Regime
