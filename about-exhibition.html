    • About Exhibition

    RENEWSEC 2025 will provide a platform to OT Cybersecurity Solutions Providers, Technology Providers, Automation & AI, Cyber Insurance Providers & Other Service Providers in Renewable Energy Sector to showcase their latest solutions & services.

    This unique platform will allow key stakeholders—including Renewable Energy Operators (Solar, Wind, Hydro & Green Hydrogen etc.), State Utilities and Regulators—to explore the latest technologies and best practices for protecting critical energy assets from emerging cyber threats.

    Why Exhibit?

    NEW CONNECTIONS

    Discover new customers and partners.

    BRANDING

    Present your company alongside leading players in the global energy ecosystem.

    THOUGHT LEADERSHIP

    Demonstrate your strategic and technical expertise.

    CUSTOMER SERVICE

    Connect with your existing customers.

    RAISE AWARENESS

    Showcase your latest products, services and solutions.

    Who Should Exhibit?

    • OT Security Providers
    • Industrial Control System (ICS) Manufacturers
    • Energy Companies (Oil & Gas, Power, Renewable, Energy Utilities)
    • Consulting Firms and Experts
    • Technology Solutions Providers
    • Hardware and Software Integrators
    • Government and Regulatory Bodies
    • Academic Institutions and Research Organizations
    • Training and Certification Providers
    • Insurance Providers
    • Automation and AI Vendors
    • Startups in Energy Sector & many more.

    Who Will You Meet There?

    • C-Level executives (CEOs, CTOs, CISOs, CFOs)
    • Heads of OT/ICS Cybersecurity, Operations, and Engineering
    • Utility plant managers and asset owners
    • Government and Regulatory Decision Makers
    • Industry Analysts & Consultants
    • Tech Integration Partners
    • Researchers and Academia
